Vidao Messenger

Vidao Messenger is an all-in-one messaging, calling, and cloud storage service. I was at Vidao Messenger for over 3 years as the UI/UX Design lead. I designed the app on Android, iOS, and the web. The most significant challenge I overcame while designing this app was to display the five main features of the app in an easy to digest way for the user. Separating the features into their own tabs was determined to be the best solution on all three platforms, with a focus on the chatting and calling experience on the web.

The app was created as a tool for university students and young professionals. It is meant to assist in everyday tasks such as sharing files, writing notes, and messaging friends. The goal was to ensure that all of these actions were easy to do within just one app, instead of having to switch around to different apps just to share a file with a friend through chat.
