Plus One

Plus One is a sports-centric event app. It allows users to search for or create sporting events for people to join. The idea arose when a small group of friends wanted to play full pitch of soccer, but could not find enough people to play. Plus One has solved this problem by giving users a platform to create events where locals can join and play with the tap of a button.
