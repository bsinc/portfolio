Vidao Connect

Vidao Connect turns Vidao Messenger into an app that revolves around the connection between a business and their customers. This app lets business owners use Vidao Messenger's great features to their advantage while gaining customers and communicating with them in unique and innovative ways.

It was challenging to take an app design that was meant for university students and young professionals and pivot into an app that would work for business owners as well. I overcame these challenges by understanding the needs of business owners while communicating to their customers, such as being able to manage their followers and contacts and communicate to specific groups of followers with ease.
