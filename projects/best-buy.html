Product Launches

At Best Buy we aim to have the latest and greatest products available for our customers as soon as possible. I have been part of a number of product launches during my time there, including Microsoft Surface, iPhone XS, MacBook Pro, and Samsung Galaxy S10. Many of these launches have a turn around time of 24 hours or less.

We work tirelessly to launch new products online with an amazing customer experience. This is a huge team effort with multiple teams across the building communicating and coordiating with each other constantly. Because of this amazing teamwork, we are often the first Canadian online retailer to have the new products live on our site.

The most daunting launches are the new flagship phones from Apple, Samsung, or Google. Because of the limited capabilities of our website the team "hacked" together a pre-order experience for these phones. This allowed the customer to not only learn more about the new phones, but choose exactly which model, colour, and carrier they wanted to buy. I helped alter existing code for every phone launch in the last year. This means I was coding HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to get this tool up and running for our customers.
